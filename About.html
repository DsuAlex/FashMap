FashMap connects people with great local Fashion Brands.

Mission

FashMap's mission is to revolutionize campus commerce by connecting college students with local student-run businesses, from fashion to personal care services like makeup, barbering, and nail art. Leveraging technology, we aim to foster a supportive ecosystem for student entrepreneurs, promoting sustainability and a vibrant campus economy.

Vision

FashMap envisions a campus where student entrepreneurship flourishes, supported by a community that values innovation and sustainability. Our platform serves as a bridge, empowering students to easily discover and support each other's businesses, enriching the university experience for everyone involved.
